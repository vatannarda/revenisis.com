From Part to Digital Twin: Precision Reverse Engineering
At Revenisis, our Reverse Engineering service transforms your physical components and assemblies into precise, editable digital models. Using a combination of mobile and stationary laser scanners alongside high-accuracy coordinate measuring machines (CMM), we capture every geometric detail as point-cloud and surface data. This raw data is then processed through industry-standard metrology software to generate CAD files that faithfully replicate the form and function of the original part.

Whether you’re looking to recreate discontinued components or optimize existing designs for enhanced performance, our engineers guide the workflow from data capture to final deliverable. We perform feature recognition, surface reconstruction and tolerance analysis to ensure that each model meets your exact specifications. By integrating functional and material properties into the CAD environment, we also enable downstream activities such as simulation, testing and process planning.

Collaboration lies at the heart of what we do. From the initial site survey through to handover of CAD deliverables, our team works closely with yours—sharing progress, insights and recommendations at every stage. Ready to move forward? Model to Machined Reality: Advanced Component and Fixture Manufacturing
At Revenisis, we transform digital models into fully functional components through our end-to-end CNC milling expertise. Starting with high-fidelity RTM prototypes or CAD deliverables, we program state-of-the-art multi-axis mills to sculpt metal and non-metal parts with micron-level precision. Every toolpath is optimized for surface finish, tight tolerances and material integrity—ensuring your conceptual designs become production-ready realities.

Our component manufacturing capabilities cover single-piece proof-of-concept runs as well as scaled batch production. From aerospace-grade alloys to engineering plastics, we select cutting strategies and spindle speeds that minimize material waste and cycle time. In-process probing and real-time feedback loops validate dimensional accuracy at every stage, delivering parts that slot seamlessly into your assembly without rework.

To complement machining, our fixture design service crafts bespoke jigs, clamps and workholding solutions tailored to your part geometry. By integrating fixture CAD with CNC toolpaths up front, we streamline setup processes and eliminate variables in mass production. Robust fixture systems not only boost throughput but also safeguard consistency across hundreds—or thousands—of repeats.

Unwavering Precision: Advanced Quality Control Solutions
When manufacturers need to verify that their parts truly meet design intent, they turn to Revenisis for expert quality control. Our advanced optical and laser scanning systems capture millions of surface points, creating a high-resolution digital twin of your physical component. We then align that scan to your original CAD model and compute every geometric deviation—down to microns—to show you precisely where tolerances are exceeded.

Our deliverables include color-coded deviation maps, comprehensive inspection reports and statistical summaries that pinpoint the magnitude and location of each variance. Armed with these insights, you can adjust machining parameters, optimize tooling strategies and eliminate costly rework. Partner with Revenisis to transform uncertainty into confidence—and keep your production running at peak efficiency. Ready to validate your parts? Contact us at info@revenisis.com to get started.
